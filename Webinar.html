Sanctuary Webinar

"Sanctuary is a theory based, trauma-informed, evidence-supported,whole culture approach that has a clear and structured methodology for creating or changing an organizational culture." - Dr. Sandra L. Bloom.

For this topic Jenn Pearce and I created a webinar to teach others about how this was being implemented in our organization.

This was webinar was created to teach community partners, families, and other organizations about what the Sanctuary Model was. Sanctuary is an evidence informed way to run an organization. Since CPRI (Child Parent Resource Institute) deals with mental health, it is important to change your way of thinking from “what is wrong with you” to “what has happened to you”. This webinar covers the theory, science and explanation for why Sanctuary is important with the work the CPRI does.

Knowledge Mobilization

A word commonly used in our department is “knowledge mobilization”. This refers to moving formal research and putting into active use. By making connections between research and practice you are able to hopefully improve outcomes in many organizations. Using the Sanctuary Model and creating this webinar is a great representation of that.
